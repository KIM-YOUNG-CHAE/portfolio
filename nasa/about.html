WHAT WE DO

At its 20 centers and facilities across the country and the only National Laboratory in space NASA studies Earth, including its climate, our Sun, and our solar system and beyond.

We conduct research, testing, and development to advance aeronautics, including electric propulsion and supersonic flight

NASA LEADERSHIP

NASA is led by Administrator Bill Nelson, who became the agency's 14th administrator on May 3, 2021. Nelson served in the U.S. Senatefor 18 years from Florida and as a payload specialist on space shuttle mission 61-C in 1986.
